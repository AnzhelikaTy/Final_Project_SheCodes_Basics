Get comfortable Choose position where you can relax, and where your breathing won't feel constricted.
Use a guided recording
or do it yourself Set a timer for desired amount of time
Start to notice
your breath Breath in and out and take note of how each inhale feels. Is it short or long? Deep or shallow?
Notice your body Ask yourself: Where do you feel your breath the most?
Reserve judgment Acknowledge the fact that your mind has wandered, don't judge it.
Assess Once your time's up, notice how you feel. Does your body feel different? Are you more relaxed?
